Instructions

To start exercising, do the following:

  1. Click on the green "Start Camera" button
  2. Wait for the application to load
  3. Select an exercise from the drop down list, and then click "Start Exercise"
  4. Position your camera according to the instruction in the yellow alert box.
  5. Start doing the exercise. Verbal hints on how to improve your form for each rep will be read out by the browser
  6. When you are done, cick on "End Exercise" to see a summary of your form. You can also click on "Show Feedback Log" so see the history of feedback on each of your reps.

About

This is the Website for the National AI Student Challenge 2022. We have used PeekingDuck as our core tool for Computer Vision.

Unfortunately, as of now, it can only support one connection due to computing constraints. The website supports both Desktop and Mobile access to work as an exercise aid.

We currently support 3 exercises: Side Squats, Front Squats, and Side Push-Ups. For best training results, please time each rep at about 2-3s.

Additionally, please ensure that the camera can see your whole body, especially your feet.

Security

Do note that by using the website, you are sending your image over to ME.

We do not store this data in any way, shape, or form, but the data is sent and processed by my computer.
