    Instructions

    Tutorial
    To start exercising, do the following:
    1. Click on the green "Start Camera" button
    2. Wait for the application to load
    3. Select an exercise from the drop down list, and then click "Start Exercise"
    4. Position your camera according to the instruction in the yellow alert box.
    5. Start doing the exercise. Verbal hints on how to improve your form for each rep will be read out by the browser
    6. When you are done, cick on "End Exercise" to see a summary of your form. You can also click on "Show Feedback Log" so see the history of feedback on each of your reps.
    Tips
    • For 'Right side' exercises, the camera should be set up directly to your right.
    • Camera should be at table height for squats and floor height for push-ups.
    • Camera angle should be vertical. For squats, the camera should be able to see your feet.
    • For best training results, please time each rep at about 2-3s.

    Information

    Application Information
    • We currently support 3 exercises: Side Squats, Front Squats, and Side Push-Ups.
    • Unfortunately, as of now, it can only support one connection due to computing constraints.
    • The website supports both Desktop and Mobile access, in portrait and landscape mode.
    • The browsers we support Chrome, Safari, and Microsoft Edge. Firefox on phone cannot be used in landscape.
    • Do note that you will be logged out after 5 minutes of inactivity while using the application.
    Data Privacy
    • Do note that by using the website, you are sending your image data to us.
    • We do not store or distribute this data in any way, shape, or form.
